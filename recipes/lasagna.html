Lasagna

Description

Delicious homemade lasagna with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese.

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 1 hour

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add minced garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Layer with 4 lasagna noodles, half of the ricotta mixture, a third of the mozzarella cheese, and a third of the meat sauce. Repeat layers, ending with meat sauce and remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  8. Let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
