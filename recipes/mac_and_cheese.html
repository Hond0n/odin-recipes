Mac and Cheese

Description

Creamy and cheesy macaroni and cheese, a classic comfort food that's easy to make and always satisfying.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 8 oz elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp paprika (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  3. Gradually whisk in milk and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat to low and stir in cheddar cheese and Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  5. Add cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir to combine. If desired, sprinkle with paprika for extra flavor.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your creamy mac and cheese!
